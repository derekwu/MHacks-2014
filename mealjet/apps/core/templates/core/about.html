About Us

  • Find yourself hungry?
  • Forgot to do grocery? Again...
  • Pick up food? Have you seen the weather...
  • Delivery? Too long & too much $$...
  • There must be a better way...

The MealJet team often found themselves in a pinch when it came to eating. In the midst of paying the price for procrastination, they had to work on projects and they didn’t have the time to go pick up food or let alone make their own. As customers, they wanted something to convenient, affordable, and healthy that doesn’t break the bank.

MealJet aims to build a community of those that need food quickly and conveniently, while also exposing fellow MealJetters to a variety of cuisines throughout the week.

Necessity is the mother of invention, and MealJet is no exception
